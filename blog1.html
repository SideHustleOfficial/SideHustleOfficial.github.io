How to Start Freelancing as a Student

Freelancing is one of the easiest ways for students to start earning online without investment.

Step 1: Choose a Skill

Writing, graphic design, video editing, coding, or social media management.

Step 2: Build a Simple Portfolio

Create 2–3 sample projects to showcase your skill.

Step 3: Join Freelancing Platforms

Start applying for beginner-level gigs and improve over time.
